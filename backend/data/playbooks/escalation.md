# Playbook: Support and Account Escalation

## Section 1: Escalation Tiers and Triggers
This playbook establishes the protocol for escalating customer issues. 
Tier 1: Standard support ticket response.
Tier 2: Escalation to the Customer Success Manager (CSM) when a critical ticket remains open for more than 24 hours or the customer expresses extreme frustration.
Tier 3: Executive sponsor involvement when there is an immediate churn risk or a contract dispute.

## Section 2: Response SLA
For Tier 2 escalations, the CSM must acknowledge the issue within 2 hours. A resolution plan must be communicated to the customer within 4 hours. 
For Tier 3 escalations, the Director of Customer Success must be notified immediately. They will schedule a call with the customer within 24 hours.

## Section 3: Post-Escalation Review
Every Tier 2 and Tier 3 escalation requires a root-cause analysis. The CS and product teams must meet to review why the issue occurred, how it was handled, and what preventative measures will be implemented to prevent future recurrences.
