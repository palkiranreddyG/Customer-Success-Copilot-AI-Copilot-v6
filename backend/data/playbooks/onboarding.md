# Playbook: Customer Onboarding

## Section 1: Overview and Objectives
Welcome to the Customer Onboarding Playbook. The onboarding phase is the most critical phase in the customer lifecycle. It spans the first 30 to 90 days after contract signing. The primary goal is to ensure a smooth transition from sales to customer success, set up initial product configurations, and guide users to their first "aha!" moment of realized value.

## Section 2: Onboarding Milestones
Every onboarding process must meet specific milestones. 
First, within 5 business days, the Customer Success Manager (CSM) must hold a Kickoff Meeting to align on goals. 
Second, within 15 days, system integration and user provisioning must be complete.
Third, within 30 days, we must conduct core team training sessions.
Fourth, by day 45, the customer should complete their first successful workflow execution.

## Section 3: Technical Setup and Verification
During technical setup, the onboarding team assists the client with setting up SSO integration, API keys, and custom data mapping. Ensure that the workspace configuration matches the client's ARR tier. Premium accounts must have dedicated sandbox environments enabled for staging.

## Section 4: Handover to Account Management
Once the customer has verified successful usage for 30 consecutive days, the CSM holds an Onboarding Graduation call. This call marks the official handover to standard account management. Update the CRM status to 'Fully Onboarded' and document any custom workflows established.
