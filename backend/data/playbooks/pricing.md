# Playbook: Pricing Tiers and Contract Expansion

## Section 1: Pricing Structure
Our product is priced based on annual recurring revenue (ARR) tiers and user counts.
Starter Tier: Up to 5 users, standard support, shared database.
Growth Tier: Up to 25 users, priority email support, isolated schemas.
Enterprise Tier: Unlimited users, 24/7 dedicated support, custom data warehouse.

## Section 2: Expansion Opportunities
Expansion refers to increasing the ARR of an existing account through upsells or cross-sells. 
CSMs should identify expansion opportunities when a customer reaches 80% of their current tier limits or maintains a health score above 85 for three consecutive months. Use the regular check-in meetings to introduce enterprise features like custom reports and audit logs.

## Section 3: Discounting Policies and Approvals
Any discounts on renewals or expansions must follow our strict approval matrix. 
Up to 10% discount: CSM can approve.
10% to 20% discount: Requires approval from the Customer Success Director.
Above 20% discount: Requires VP of Sales or CFO approval.
Never promise discounts before obtaining the necessary internal approvals.
