# Customer Health Playbook

## Health Score Guidelines

Customer health is measured using product adoption, support experience, executive engagement, and business outcomes.

### Healthy Account

Health Score: 80-100

Characteristics:

- High product adoption
- Positive executive engagement
- Few support tickets
- Quarterly business reviews completed
- Renewal confidence is high

Recommended Actions:

- Identify expansion opportunities.
- Introduce advanced platform capabilities.
- Schedule executive roadmap reviews.

---

### Moderate Risk

Health Score: 60-79

Characteristics:

- Reduced product usage
- Delayed onboarding milestones
- Increased support requests
- Limited executive participation

Recommended Actions:

- Conduct customer success review.
- Schedule product training.
- Monitor adoption weekly.

---

### High Risk

Health Score: Below 60

Characteristics:

- Declining product adoption
- Executive dissatisfaction
- Multiple unresolved tickets
- Renewal uncertainty

Required Actions:

- Escalate to Customer Success Director.
- Schedule executive business review.
- Create a 30-day recovery plan.
- Assign a dedicated success manager.
